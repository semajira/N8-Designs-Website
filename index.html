How It Works

From Brief to Brand

No bloated timelines, no confusing back-and-forth. Just a clear path from first conversation to finished product in your hands.

01

Tell Us About Your Brand

We start with a conversation. Share your brand, your vision, and what you want to create. We'll figure out the right products, quantities, and direction together — no pressure, no minimum-order pitch.

02

We Design It

Our team develops concepts built around your brand identity — not generic templates. Expect bold ideas, honest feedback, and a collaborative process until the design is exactly right.

03

We Make It Real

From production management and quality control to final delivery, we handle everything end-to-end. You focus on running your brand — we'll handle the rest.

About N8 Designs

Design-First.
Production-Ready.

N8 Designs is a Vancouver-based merchandise studio built for brands that don't fit the standard retail mold. We work closely with our clients — from initial concept through final delivery — to create merchandise that earns a place in people's wardrobes and on their shelves.

Whether it's a cocktail bar launching its first merch run, a craft distillery building brand equity, or a neighbourhood business ready to turn regulars into ambassadors — we bring the same level of craft and intention to every project.
